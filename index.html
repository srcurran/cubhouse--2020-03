Our commitment to safety

Last Updated March 20, 2020

On March 19 Governor Newsom issued a statewide Stay at Home order to confront the growing coronavirus outbreak in California. Residents are ordered to stay at home except for essential needs and should practice social distancing in public. You can learn more here

To help those in our community who use a bicycle as their primary mode of transportation, or for exercise, The Cub House is remaining open for essential bike service only. The safety of our employees and customers is, and will continue to be, our top priority as we navigate this uncertain time. In order to provide this service in a safe environment we have implemented strict new protocols for employees within the work space and for our interactions with customers. We ask that you review all the information on this website carefully and respect the plan that we’ve outlined.

Any and all of this can change at any time. We will keep this space up-to-date with our current procedures and you can follow us on instagram @itsnotabikeshop for updates.

What we need from you:

  • Make an appointment.
  • Don’t show up if you’re sick at all, in any way.
  • Learn and respect the current operating procedures.

Our retail store is currently closed, but at this time we are still shipping online orders. All orders within the USA are shipped free of charge. Visit the online store at teamdreamteam.com

What to expect when you arrive

Repair Drop-off and Pick-up

  • You must have an appointment, we are not offering drop-in service at this time. Contact us via text/call (626) 755-3003 or email info@cubhouse.cc to set up a time.
  • Show up on time for your appointment. If you are late, or fail to show, you must contact us to reschedule.
  • When you arrive, wait outside the front gate. We will meet you there to discuss your repair while maintaining a minimum 6ft distance.
  • If you are dropping off a bike we ask that you place it in the stand provided and we’ll take it from there.
  • If you are picking up a bike we will place it back in the same stand outside the gate after you have paid.

Generally

  • Appointments help us limit the number of people on-site, reducing the risk of exposure to our employees and customers. Make an appointment.
  • At this time no customers are allowed to enter the front gate. If you require parts with your service we will bring out options to show you.
  • Please, no cash payments. To limit contact we’d prefer you pay prior to pickup. We will email you an invoice to be paid online when your bike is ready, but if necessary we accept apple pay or credit card on-site.
  • Our employees are regularly washing hands, wearing gloves and cleaning/disinfecting surfaces. We are limiting the number of staff and practicing social distancing on-site.
  • We reserve the right to refuse service to anyone who does not abide by the safety precautions we’ve outlined here.
